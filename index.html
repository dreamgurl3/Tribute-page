The Legend of Jackie Chan (Chan Kong-Sang)

Jackie Chan is a Hong Kong born actor, director, writer, producer, martial artist, singer, stunt man... 

Below is a timeline of Jacki's life events and his major achievement:

  • 1954 - Born in British Hong Kong
  • 1960 - Moved to Canberra Australia
  • 1961 - He was sent back to Hong Kong to attend a boarding school due to his unsuccessful schooling in Australia
  • 1961 until 1971 - He remained at the bording school until he turned 17 years old where he learned acrobatics, singing, martial arts, and mime—skills
  • 1972 - He got a minor role in a Bruce Lee's movie called "Fist of Fury"
  • 1972 until 1975 - He started to appear in many low budget martial arts movies after the death of Bruce Lee
  • 1978 - He was starred in "Drunken Master" which was his breakout role
  • 1980 - He directed his first movie called "The Young Master"
  • 1982 - He got married to Feng-Jiao Lin, an actress from Taiwan
  • 1982 - His son was born shortly after their marriage
  • 1986 - He fractured his skull while filmingArmour of God, one of his many major injuries throughout his filming career
  • 1995 - His first successful U.S. movie called "Rumble in the Bronx" which opened up the door for him in Hollywood
  • 1980s until present - he has been doing charity works including providing financial assistant to youth, working with UNICEF, and more...
  • 1980 until present - he won 49 awards around the world
  • 1980 until present - He has been acting and producing movies for his entire career and just recently he is not doing anymore stunts himself
